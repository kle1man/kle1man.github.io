My Journey

As a psychology major with a passion for medicine, I'm on a unique path that combines mental health understanding with medical aspirations. My journey began in a different field, but through personal experiences and academic exploration, I discovered my true calling in medicine.

Currently, I serve as the Co-President of Pre-SOMA at the University of Maryland, where I work to create opportunities for fellow students interested in osteopathic medicine. This leadership role has strengthened my commitment to building community in healthcare education.

I'm in the early stages of my pre-medical journey, eager to gain clinical experience and research opportunities while maintaining a strong academic foundation in psychology and pre-medical sciences.
